Editorial Integrity & Review Process

At Mind & Balance, we adhere to the highest standards of scientific communication. Our mission is to provide clinical accuracy that empowers our readers to make informed decisions about their neurological and psychological well-being.

The 4-Stage Clinical Review

1. Scientific Selection

Every topic is selected based on its relevance to the current body of peer-reviewed research. We prioritize queries from the Nature Neuroscience and APA clinical databases.

2. Expert Auditing

Articles are drafted by experienced science communicators and subsequently audited by our Medical Review Board (led by Dr. Aris) for technical accuracy and clinical safety.

3. Integrity Checklist

We verify all citations against the NIH and PubMed databases. Any content found to be speculative or unsupported by biological consensus is flagged for revision or removal.

4. Periodic Re-Validation

Science evolves. Our "Authority Hubs" are reviewed semi-annually to ensure that they reflect the latest developments in neuro-plasticity and behavioral genetics.
